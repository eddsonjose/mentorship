Catherine Thy Le was born in Southern Caifornia. From a young age, I was interested and found myself becoming immersed in the arts. I started off with simple pencil sketches and worked my way up to different mediums like ink, watercolor, and acrylic paint. As I entered high school, I started to take my traditional art more seriously and was also intorduced to the worlds of design and digital artwork. Now, I am persuing a BFA in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University and working on learning more about digital art.

As an artist, I don't limit myself to what I can do. Digital and traditional mediums are all fair game for me. I use mediums that I find interesting, those in which I would like to hone in my skills with. The same can be said for my subject matter. I create artwork based off of what catches my eye. A recreation of landscapes, faces, objects, all through my own lense. With my art, I want to provide the viewer an emotional experience- one where they feel happiness, peace, sadness, shock, anger, awe. I belive the beauty of art lies within how it able to connect complete strangers regardless of their identity and background and I would like to provide that same space with my own art in the future.
